Full Guide: How To Play Trixcy's Virtual Garden 📖

This is your complete walkthrough so every feature is easy to enjoy.

1. Start The Experience

On the landing screen, tap Yes, that's me 💕 to enter the magical garden.

2. Move Around The Garden

Use keyboard controls W A S D or Arrow Keys to walk around.

On mobile, use the movement pad in the lower-left corner.

3. Interact With Flowers

Tap any flower to select it, then use:

- Water Flower 💧 to grow bloom stages

- Plant New Flower 🌱 to create more flowers

When a flower fully blooms, it reveals:

- Love note

- Memory

- Special message

- Secret compliment

4. Explore Special Places

- Memory Tree 🌳: opens timeline memories and milestones

- Love Letter House 💌: opens letters with typewriter animation

- Wishing Fountain ⛲: click to throw a coin and reveal dreams/goals

- Daily Flower 🌷: reveals a fresh surprise each day

5. Use Music Garden

Open Music Garden 🎵 and do the following:

- Upload songs

- Play/Pause, Next, Previous

- Toggle Shuffle and Repeat

- Adjust volume

The visualizer and floating notes animate while music plays.

6. Open Photo Blossom Gallery

Tap Photo Blossom 🌹 in the top bar.

- Upload photos

- Add captions

- Tap any image to open lightbox view

- Use slideshow mode for continuous viewing

7. Day And Night Mode

Use Switch to Night ✨ to toggle romantic night mode with stars and fireflies.

8. Find Hidden Areas

Walk around and discover:

- Rose Tunnel

- Secret Heart Cave

- Hidden Sakura Garden

- Dream Lake

Exploration unlocks achievements.

9. Butterfly Messenger

Tap butterflies while they fly to reveal random sweet messages, memories, and confessions.

10. Track Progress

Open Achievements 🏆 to see unlocked milestones.

Watch the top counter for Since Courtship live time progression.

11. Admin Dashboard (Local)

Open Admin Dashboard, enter passcode, then you can:

- Add flower content

- Add letters

- Add memory branches

- Change theme accent

- Monitor garden statistics

Quick Goal Checklist

- Bloom at least 10 flowers

- Click butterflies

- Visit all hidden areas

- Upload songs and listen to all of them

- Add photos and run slideshow

- Read memory timeline and love letters
